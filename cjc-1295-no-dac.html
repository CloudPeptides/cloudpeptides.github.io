Research Overview

Overview

CJC-1295 No DAC is a synthetic growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog commonly studied for its ability to stimulate endogenous growth hormone release through GHRH receptor activation.

Researchers investigate its effects on growth hormone physiology, IGF-1 signaling, body composition, recovery, and endocrine function in laboratory settings.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

CJC-1295 No DAC binds to GHRH receptors in the pituitary, promoting pulsatile growth hormone release. Scientists continue studying how this influences downstream IGF-1 production and metabolic physiology.
