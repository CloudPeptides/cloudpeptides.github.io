Research Overview

Overview

AOD-9604 is a modified fragment of human growth hormone investigated primarily for metabolic and adipose tissue research. Scientists study how this fragment may influence lipid metabolism while acting differently from full-length growth hormone.

Research continues to explore its role in body composition, energy balance, and metabolic physiology.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

Researchers investigate AOD-9604 for its potential interactions with pathways involved in fat metabolism and adipose tissue biology rather than traditional growth-promoting effects associated with full-length growth hormone.
