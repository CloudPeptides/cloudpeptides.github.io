Research Overview

Overview

CJC-1295 No DAC and Ipamorelin are frequently investigated together because they influence separate pathways involved in endogenous growth hormone release. Researchers study this combination to better understand growth hormone physiology and downstream IGF-1 signaling.

Current research explores applications involving body composition, recovery biology, endocrine regulation, and exercise adaptation.
