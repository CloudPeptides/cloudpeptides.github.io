Research Overview

Overview

PT-141, also known as Bremelanotide, is a synthetic melanocortin peptide investigated for neuroendocrine signaling and reproductive biology. Research focuses on central melanocortin pathways and receptor-mediated endocrine communication.

Laboratory studies explore PT-141 in models involving melanocortin receptor biology, neuroendocrine regulation, behavioral neuroscience, and reproductive physiology.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

Researchers investigate PT-141 for its interaction with melanocortin receptors, particularly within central nervous system pathways involved in neuroendocrine signaling and hormone-related biology.
