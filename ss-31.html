Research Overview

Overview

SS-31 is a mitochondria-targeted peptide studied for its relationship to mitochondrial membrane biology, oxidative stress, and cellular energy production. It is often discussed in scientific literature under the name Elamipretide.

Researchers investigate SS-31 in laboratory models involving mitochondrial dysfunction, cardiometabolic biology, neurobiology, skeletal muscle physiology, and healthy aging.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

SS-31 is studied for its interaction with cardiolipin, a phospholipid found in the inner mitochondrial membrane. Researchers investigate how this interaction may influence mitochondrial structure, electron transport, oxidative stress, and cellular energy production.
