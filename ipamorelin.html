Research Overview

Overview

Ipamorelin is a synthetic growth hormone secretagogue studied for its selective activity at the ghrelin receptor. Researchers investigate how this pathway influences endogenous growth hormone release and downstream endocrine signaling.

It is commonly studied in relation to recovery biology, exercise adaptation, body composition, and growth hormone physiology.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

Ipamorelin activates the growth hormone secretagogue receptor, also known as the ghrelin receptor. Researchers study how this activation may influence pulsatile growth hormone release and related IGF-1 signaling.
