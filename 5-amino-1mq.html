Research Overview

Overview

5-Amino-1MQ is a research compound studied primarily for its ability to inhibit nicotinamide N-methyltransferase (NNMT), an enzyme involved in cellular metabolism and energy regulation.

Researchers investigate how modulation of NNMT activity may influence adipose tissue biology, metabolic efficiency, and body composition in laboratory models.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

Current research focuses on inhibition of NNMT, an enzyme associated with nicotinamide metabolism. Scientists continue to investigate how changes in this pathway affect cellular energy utilization and metabolic function.
