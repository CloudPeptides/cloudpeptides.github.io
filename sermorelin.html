Research Overview

Overview

Sermorelin is a synthetic analog of growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH). Researchers investigate its ability to stimulate endogenous growth hormone release through activation of the GHRH receptor.

Published research explores its role in endocrine physiology, IGF-1 biology, body composition, recovery, and healthy aging.

Mechanism of Action

How It Works

Sermorelin activates GHRH receptors within the pituitary gland, promoting physiologic growth hormone release. Scientists continue studying downstream effects on IGF-1 production and metabolic function.
