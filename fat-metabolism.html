Fat Metabolism

Explore how the body stores, mobilizes, and utilizes fat for energy. This chapter summarizes the major pathways involved in lipid metabolism and adipose tissue biology.

Research Topic

Lipolysis

Lipolysis is the breakdown of stored triglycerides into free fatty acids and glycerol. Researchers study the hormones and enzymes that regulate this process during fasting, exercise, and energy demand.
